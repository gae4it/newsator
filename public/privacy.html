Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 10, 2024

1. Introduction

Newsator AI is a student project created for educational and research purposes only. This application demonstrates the integration of AI-powered news aggregation using Google Gemini API.

2. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from users. This application:

  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not use analytics or tracking tools
  • Does not require user registration or login
  • Does not store any user preferences or settings
  • Does not collect IP addresses or device information

3. Third-Party Services

This application uses the following third-party service:

  • Google Gemini API - For AI-powered news aggregation and summarization

News content is fetched in real-time through the Gemini API with Google Search grounding. We do not store or cache any news content on our servers.

This project is developed solely for educational purposes as part of a study project. It is not intended for commercial use or public distribution.

5. No Warranties

This application is provided "as is" without any warranties, express or implied. News content accuracy and availability depend on third-party services.

6. Contact
