Install Newsator AI App

Get the full experience. Newsator AI App works as a Progressive Web App (PWA), meaning you can install it on your device without using an app store.

Android

  1. 1. Open in Chrome or Edge.
  2. 2. Click the install icon in the Newsator AI Navigation Bar.
  3. 3. Follow the prompt to install.

Windows / Mac

  1. 1. Open in Chrome or Edge.
  2. 2. Click the install icon in the Newsator AI Navigation Bar.
  3. 3. Follow the prompt to install.

iPhone / iPad

  1. 1. Open in Safari browser.
  2. 2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow).
  3. 3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
